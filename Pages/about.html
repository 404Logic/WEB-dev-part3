About Spares & Brothers

The story of two brothers building a trusted local business

Our Story

Spares & Brothers was born in 2021 from the passion and dedication of two brothers from Vleifontein, Limpopo. What started as a simple backyard hobby of fixing cars gradually transformed into a thriving local business that serves our community with honesty and quality.

Growing up, we spent countless hours in our backyard, learning the intricacies of automotive repair and developing a deep appreciation for quality workmanship. This hands-on experience, combined with our professional backgrounds in Mechanical Engineering and Information Technology, has shaped our approach to business.

Today, we're proud to offer our community a trusted source for quality used vehicles and genuine spare parts, backed by the personal service and integrity that only a family-owned business can provide.
